How can I track my order?
After placing an order, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our website under the "Track Order" section. If you experience any issues, our customer support team is available to assist you.
What is your return and refund policy?
We offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of purchase, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be credited to your original payment method.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on your location, which you can check during checkout. Please note that customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country’s regulations.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with encryption to protect your information. We do not store your payment details on our servers.
Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement by contacting our support team. After this period, orders are processed and shipped, making cancellations difficult. If your order has already shipped, you can request a return once you receive the item.
